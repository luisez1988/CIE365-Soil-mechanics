Class objective 1

[O1]: Develop an understanding of soil phase relations, index properties, and their application to soil classification and compaction
Class outcomes:
  • Describe some soil deposits and their characteristics in the context of a case scenario.
  • Calculate the bulk, dry, saturated, and buoyant unit weights.
  • Calculate the water content and the degree of saturation of soils.
  • Calculate the void ratio and porosity of the soils.
  • Plot GSD and calculate soil fractions, particle sizes, and the coefficients of gradation and curvature.
  • Calculate the Atterberg limits.
  • Calculate the activity of soils and relate it with its mineralogy.
  • Classify the soil using the USCS and AASHTO systems.
In a scale from 0 to 100, being 100 equivalent to fully agree and 0 fully disagree; Did you developed an understanding of soil phase relations, index properties, and their application to soil classification and compaction?
